# PRODUCT.md

## Register
product

## Product purpose
Pipeline is the lead-to-install sales tracker for a custom-closets business. Reps log
leads and move them through the funnel (new, consult, quote, won, lost). The operational
data lands in Microsoft Fabric and feeds analytics. The app's job: make "who do I follow
up with today, and is the pipeline healthy" answerable in one glance, and make logging a
lead a ten-second task.

## Users
Design consultants and showroom reps. Not analysts. They open the app between client
meetings on a laptop, scan, act, and leave. Low patience for chrome.

## Scene
A design consultant at a custom-closets showroom, mid-morning between client meetings,
scanning their pipeline on a laptop to decide who to chase. Daytime, professional, calm.
This forces a light theme.

## Tone
Trustworthy, calm, enterprise-grade. Quiet confidence over flash. The tool disappears
into the task.

## Anti-references
- Flashy SaaS gradients and hero-metric dashboards.
- Closets and home-org cliches: wood textures, beige, "cozy" warmth.
- Toy-like or playful CRM aesthetics.

## Strategic principles
- The board is the product. The pipeline is visible the moment you land.
- Every stage move records a stage event. The UI's actions are the analytics' raw data.
- Density with breathing room. Many leads, scannable, never cramped.
